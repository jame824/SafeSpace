About Us

Our Mission

SafeSpace's mission is to provide a space with resources for underrepresented communities to learn about and embrace their mental health.

Our Vision

It is our hope to destigmatize mental health in underrepresented communities and act as a medium to help.

Purpose

Research shows that in diverse populations across the US, dispiarites in mental health are widespread. Due to factors such as discrimination, cultural stigma, lack of awareness, and inaccessibility to mental health services, underrepresented minorities in the US live in adverse conditions everyday.

Let's look at a few communities. According to the American Psychiatric Association, 2.7 million AAPI have a mental illness or disorder. In the Black and African American community, Mental Health America reports that 4.8 million people reported having a mental illness.

So how does SafeSpace fit into all this? By working with voices in the community, providing an extensive resource library catered to respective communities, and ways to connect with others, we hope to act as a catalyst towards healing and change in the communities we are apart of.
